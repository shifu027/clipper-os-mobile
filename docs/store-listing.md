## FULL DESCRIPTION — Google Play (max 4000 chars)

```
Clipper OS is the all-in-one content management tool built for social media creators, editors, and clippers who need to stay organised across multiple platforms.

📋 DASHBOARD
Get a quick snapshot of your workflow at a glance. See how many content items are in each stage, your recent activity, and what needs attention today.

📚 LIBRARY
Your central content hub. Store and organise all your video ideas, clips, and content pieces. Tag them, categorise them, and find exactly what you need in seconds.

✂️ CLIPS MANAGER
Track every clip you've created or plan to create. Log clip details, mark status (draft, ready, published), and keep your production pipeline moving smoothly.

🔄 PIPELINE
A visual kanban-style board to move your content from idea → in production → review → published. Drag and drop items across stages to reflect your real workflow.

📊 PUBLISHING HISTORY
Never lose track of what you've published, when, and where. Export your full history as a CSV for reporting, invoicing, or portfolio management.

🤖 AI STUDIO
A dedicated space for AI-assisted content ideation. Available in future updates — the studio is thoughtfully built to keep your API key secure via backend proxy, never exposed client-side.

---

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
Clipper OS is 100% offline. No account required. No data ever leaves your device. All your content data is stored locally using your device's secure storage.

📱 BUILT FOR MOBILE
Designed from the ground up for phone use — fast, responsive, and works great on both Android and iOS.

⚡ NO SUBSCRIPTIONS
Download once, use forever. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no ads.

---

Perfect for:
• YouTube clippers and short-form video editors
• Social media managers handling multiple clients
• Content creators who want to organise their backlog
• Freelancers tracking deliverables and publishing schedules

Download Clipper OS and take control of your content workflow today.
```
